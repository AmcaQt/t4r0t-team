About the Order

Small crew.
Deadly precision.

T4R0T is a Malaysian cybersecurity CTF team forged by those who see beyond the surface — like reading between the cards. We specialize in real-world attack scenarios, reverse engineering the unknown, and finding flags others miss.

Founded by AmcaQt, our team competes across global platforms, documents every discovery, and gives knowledge back to the community.
